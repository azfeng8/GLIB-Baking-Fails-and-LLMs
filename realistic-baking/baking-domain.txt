Example domain description:

In the domain, the robot is tasked to bake desserts, starts in front of the kitchen counter, where the following raw ingredients are prepared on the counter:

- A stick of butter, unwrapped, and cut into chunks by tablespoon, sitting on a piece of parchment paper.
- A quarter tablespoon of cream of tartar, a white powder used for baking, in a small cup.
- Two cups of all-purpose wheat flour in a measuring cup.
- A large purple mug with 1 cup of white sugar.
- A large yellow mug with 1 cup of white sugar.
- One whole, organic, brown raw egg in its shell in the egg carton.
- One whole, organic, white raw egg in its shell in the egg carton.

The following kitchen items are also on the counter:

- A medium-sized red ceramic bowl.
- A medium-sized blue ceramic bowl.
- A medium-sized nonstick orange pan.
- A medium-sized nonstick green pan.
- A stand mixer.

Next to the sink, there are cleaning items:
- A small bottle of dish soap, containing just enough soap to wash {insert number} medium-sized items.
- A stack of disposable paper towels (each towel needs to be an object).

The following kitchen appliances are nearby:
- A conventional oven, located behind and to the right of the counter.
- An electric stove, located behind and to the left of the counter.

There are hypothetical objects which may or may not exist at different states. Each hypothetical object has a predicate that asserts if that object exists or not.

(:action clean-mixer
:parameters (?m - mixer ?t - paper_towel)
:precondition (and
    (wipe-mixer(?m ?t))
    (not (mixer-is-clean ?m))
    (not (towel-is-used ?t))
)
:effect (and
    (mixer-is-clean ?m)
    (towel-is-used ?t)
)
; skill (wipe-mixer ?m ?t)
)

(:action clean-bowl
:parameters (?b - container ?s - soap_hypothetical)
:precondition (and
    (clean ?b ?s)
    (is-bowl ?b)
    (not (soap-consumed ?s))
    (not (container-on-a-stove ?b))
    (not (container-in-an-oven ?b))
)
:effect (and
    (soap-consumed ?s)
    (container-is-clean ?b)
)
; skill (clean ?b ?s)
)
